Alt Fine foods is Buffalo's only all-kosher caterer. We're ready to cater for your simcha, tour group, or even help when you want to sposor a kiddush in your Synagogue. We are a strictly kosher catering company, capable of providing either dairy or meat menus.

Alt Fine Foods is ready to cater for any Simcha. Whether it's a Wedding, Bar Mitzvah or Bat Mitzvah, or the Bris Milah of your new child, we can provide you with the brunch, dinner, or even Shabbaton or Melave Malka that will keep your friends and family talking. Just click on one of the links above for a sample menu, or contact us to start creating a menu just for you. You may also want to check out some of our kiddush options as well.
Alt Fine foods is under the supervision of Va'ad HaKashrut of Buffalo, which is run by Rabbi Moshe Taub.
